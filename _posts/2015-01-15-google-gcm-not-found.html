It simply says that I lost the Google GCM(Google Cloud Messaging) SDK. The normal way to solve this is to look into the SDK manager and download the required SDK. It should be in the Extras folder but what I saw was this:



There seems to be no GCM, right? Then I searched on Google and find the answer. 


Simply tick the button called Obsolete.
